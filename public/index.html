Stop 01

El Saler & L’Albufera

Our base for the weekend. This natural reserve is one of the most special landscapes around Valencia: sea, dunes, pine trees and rice fields all in one place.

Useful note: this is where the hotel will be, so most transfers will start or end here.

Stop 02

City center

The historical heart of Valencia: elegant streets, beautiful architecture and many of the classic spots to stroll, have a drink and enjoy the city.

Useful note: ideal area for a walk, lunch, or a first feel of Valencia if you arrive early.

Stop 03

El Cabanyal

One of the areas closest to our hearts. It keeps Valencia’s maritime spirit alive and is full of local character, history and sea breeze.

Useful note: a great area for seaside walks, rice dishes and a more local atmosphere.

Stop 04

Masía Xamandreu

The setting for the celebration itself. A beautiful Valencian estate with exactly the kind of atmosphere we wanted for the pedida.

Useful note: final logistics, timing and transport details can sit here later.

Stop 05

Rocafort

A place with deep family meaning and part of Olimpia’s story. It adds a very personal layer to the weekend and to this map.

Useful note: you can keep this as a personal family note or expand it later.

About Valencia

A few things to know

Valencia is one of those cities that feels both elegant and relaxed: historic center, sea, wide avenues, good food, and an easy pace. It is particularly special for us because it brings together family history, personal memories, and the Mediterranean setting we love.
